How to Play

To start playing, follow these simple steps:

Start a new quiz: Click the "Start New Quiz" button on the home page to begin a new quiz session.

Answer the questions: Read the questions carefully and select the correct answer. Each correct answer will earn you points.

Check the high scores: After completing the quiz, you can visit the "High Scores" page to see the top 5 players and their scores.

Read the instructions: If you need more information on how to play, click the "Instructions" button to access these helpful guidelines.

Select Category and Difficulty: When you start the game, you will see a form that allows you to choose a category and difficulty level. Select the topic you want to be quizzed on and the difficulty level that suits your preference. Click the "Submit" button to proceed to the game.

Answer the Questions: Once the game area is loaded, you will see a question displayed on the screen. Read the question carefully and select the correct answer from the available choices. Each correct answer will earn you points, which will be displayed in the "Score" section of the heads-up display (HUD).

Progress through the Quiz: As you answer questions, the progress bar in the HUD will fill up, indicating your progress through the quiz. The "Question" section of the HUD will show the current question number.

Check High Scores: After completing the quiz, you can navigate to the "High Scores" page to see the top 5 players and their scores. This will allow you to compare your performance with other players and strive to achieve a higher score.

When you navigate to the High Scores page, you will see the following elements: Best Players Heading: This is the main title of the page, indicating that the content displayed are the best players and their scores. High Scores List: An unordered list will be displayed, showing the top 5 players and their respective scores. The scores will be presented in a centered format for easy readability. Go Home Link: At the bottom of the page, you will find a link that allows you to navigate back to the home page of the ChecKnowledge game.
